Will COVID-19 be gone any sooner?

by K.A.B Esteban

May 30, 2023

What should we do if we are on lockdown again? We all should know that just because the lockdown has loosen their restrictions, it doesn't mean that the pandemic is over. Throughout the years, the government has implemented restrictions and regulations regarding the COVID-19 lockdown. With the removal of mandatory face shields and face masks on public, the risk of the spread might return.

Should we worry about it? No. The majority of the population are already vaccinated and so it means that we are getting lower chances of spreading the virus. However, we should not be confident as some variants of the virus can resist the effects of the vaccine. If you are experiencing the common symptoms of COVID-19, head out to the nearest testing center and always take care of yourself.
